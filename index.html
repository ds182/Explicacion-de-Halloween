What is Halloween? (English)

Halloween is celebrated on October 31st, and it is a time when people dress up in costumes, go trick-or-treating, and decorate their homes with spooky themes. The tradition originated from the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain, where people believed that ghosts of the dead returned to earth. Nowadays, Halloween is a fun event filled with parties, scary movies, and lots of candy.
